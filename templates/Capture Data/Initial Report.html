NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of [date] (the "Effective Date") between [investigator's name and contact information] (the "Investigator") and [client's name and contact information] (the "Client").

WHEREAS, the Client has engaged the Investigator to provide investigative services in connection with certain matters (the "Matters"); and

WHEREAS, the Investigator may have access to confidential and proprietary information of the Client in the course of providing such services.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

  1. Confidential Information.

    "Confidential Information" means any and all information, whether oral, written or in electronic form, that is disclosed by the Client to the Investigator in connection with the Matters, and which is marked as confidential or proprietary or which the Investigator should reasonably understand to be confidential or proprietary. Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to, trade secrets, financial information, customer lists, and any other information that the Client designates as confidential.

  2. Non-Disclosure.

    The Investigator agrees to use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of providing the investigative services to the Client and to keep the Confidential Information confidential and not to disclose or use the Confidential Information, directly or indirectly, for any other purpose without the prior written consent of the Client.

  3. Exclusions.

    This Agreement shall not apply to Confidential Information that:

    1. Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Investigator;
    2. Was in the Investigator's possession prior to receipt from the Client, as evidenced by the Investigator's written records;
    3. Is received from a third party who lawfully acquired the information and who is under no obligation of confidentiality to the Client; or
    4. Is required to be disclosed by law, court order or governmental regulation; provided, however, that the Investigator shall provide the Client with prompt written notice of such requirement in order to allow the Client the opportunity to seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy.
  4. Return of Confidential Information.

    Upon the completion of the investigative services or at any time upon the Client's request, the Investigator shall return all Confidential Information to the Client or destroy such Confidential Information and certify in writing that it has been destroyed.

  5. Remedies.

    The parties agree that any breach of this Agreement may result in irreparable harm to the Client and that the Client shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, in addition to any other remedies that may be available at law or in equity.

  6. Governing Law.

    This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of theState of [state], without regard to conflicts of law principles.

  7. Entire Agreement.

    This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous agreements, whether written or oral.

  8. Modification.

    This Agreement may not be amended or modified except in writing signed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date. Capodilupo Investigation's.
